CrossSwitch 1.5
- In CrossSwitch you have a board of blue spaces, turn the blue
spaces green by tapping them. 

- Win by making all spaces green.

- There are are 5 different layouts to choose from

- CrossSwitch tracks how many moves a player uses to complete
a layout, and displays their best on the main page.
Try to beat your best score.
